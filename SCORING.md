# Scoring

Points are awarded for **bad** performance. Good players score negative; bad players score positive.

---

## Batting

### Outs
Points increase with more outs. The 4th out in a game triggers a bonus.

| Outs | Points |
|------|--------|
| 1    | +0.75  |
| 2    | +1.50  |
| 3    | +2.25  |
| 4    | +4.00 (+1 bonus) |
| 5    | +5.00  |

> Strikeouts count as outs AND score additional K points (see below).

### Hits
Penalty increases with more hits in a game.

| Hits | Points |
|------|--------|
| 1    | -1.50  |
| 2    | -3.50  |
| 3    | -6.00  |
| 4    | -8.50  |

### Strikeouts
Bonus stacks on top of the out. Escalates sharply with volume.

| K | Points (K bonus only) |
|---|----------------------|
| 1 | +0.50  |
| 2 | +1.00  |
| 3 | +3.00  |
| 4 | +7.00  |
| 5 | +13.00 |

## Pitching

### Innings Pitched
-1.80 per inning (-.6 per out achieved). A 6-inning start scores -10.8.

### Strikeouts
Bonus for low-K games; penalty for efficient strikers.

| K total | Per additional K |
|---------|-----------------|
| 1–7     | -0.50           |
| 8–9     | -1.00           |
| 10+     | -2.00           |

### Home Runs Allowed

| HR | Points |
|----|--------|
| 1st | +3.00 |
| 2nd | +3.50 |
| 3rd+ | +4.00 |
